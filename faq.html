Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Digital Empower, our initiatives, and how citizens can benefit from digital services.

What is Digital Empower? +
Digital Empower is an initiative aimed at providing digital tools, skills, and opportunities to citizens across India to promote inclusive digital growth.
Who can use Digital Empower resources? +
Students, professionals, entrepreneurs, rural citizens, and anyone interested in improving digital literacy can use our resources.
Are the learning resources free? +
Yes, most of the digital literacy courses and resources are freely accessible to ensure equal opportunities for all citizens.
How does this platform support Digital India? +
The platform aligns with the Digital India mission by promoting e-governance, digital infrastructure, and digital empowerment nationwide.
How can I contact the Digital Empower team? +
You can reach us through the Contact page by submitting the form, and our team will respond as soon as possible.
